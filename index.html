Warning!
Important information

This BMI calculator is for use by adults over 20 years old.

BMI calculations cannot take into account:

  • Age
  • Sex
  • Muscle mass or Body fat
  • Pregnancy Status

Athletes or very muscular adults may be classed "overweight" or "obese" even though they have a lower level body fat.

Older people may have a low BMI but this may be due to having less muscle and more harmful body fat.

Pregnant women should use their pre-pregnancy weight when calculating their BMI.

Your BMI score will fall into one of the four categories below.


BMI
Underweight Below 18.5
Normal 18.5–24.9
Overweight 25.0–29.9
Obesity 30.0 and Above
